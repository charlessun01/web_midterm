STRATEGIES

Things to know on your voyage

-The ship must move from where it begins to where it can
continue no more for the crew to win

-An iceberg will block your travel, sometimes you WILL hit
an iceburg by accident, it normally creats three leaks. Leaks can
be repaired with one wood and one nail.

-Polar bears are not to be underestimated

-The blizzard will arrive at the end of the 4th day,
when you can no longer warm you'll know it's upon you

-Thralls can decrease their ability's cooldown by building TOTEMS

-A totem cost 3 wood and 1 bone to make

Strategy- The Good Crew

-Everyone works together in their role, trying their best to trust
fellow crew. Sail the ship as far as possible on the first day by
collecting nearby coal and continuing post haste.

-Works as one, feed and arm those that are trustworthy.

-Band together to collect the nitro, crush any crew that have turned INSANE

Strategy- The Two Factions

-Split up into two groups, one takes care of the boat, one collects,
gathers, and when the time is right goes after the nitro

-If one group perils the other can support -This puts pressure in two ways

Strategy- The Free For All

-Distrust is high amongst the crew

-The crew is inexperienced in working together with their roles

-Keep yourself and those that are motivated to survive, alive

-Be as efficient as possible and make necessary sacrifices, hopefully
one of us makes it home to tell our story.

'Do not go gentle into that good night,
Old age should burn and rave at close of day;
Rage, rage against the dying of the light'

Thrall Strategies

The deciever
Stays with the crew and subvert them where you can, slowing them down
at every turn, bide your time right up until the last moment , reveal
yourself once the situation is dire.

The creature
Abandon the crew early and build up your power and weaponry. Use your
stockpiles of supplies to torment the crew. After whittling down the
innocent, force a final encounter.

The insane
Make early for the nitro, use the time to create a plan, beware the crew may
seek an alternate method of escape.

Minor Deceptions

-Fuel arguments with paranoid crew members

-Frame someone by planting contraband

-Create a "Doubt Card"
Keep a loyal crew with you, create a bond, call upon then to vouch
for your previous actions

-An explosion in the front of the ship can look like you hit something
