The Captain

The leader of the voyage, keeps the crew on task, challenges suspicious behaviour, takes care of the ship, collects supplies where possible.

Starting item: Cutlass(melee weapon), Tea

Primary:
Good at gaurding the boat
Good at steering but can delegate the tast to another

Secondary: collecting/cooking/crafting etc

Perk: able to maneuver the ship better

Thrall Tactics

-sabotage the boat
-blame the navigator
-sail poorly
-when left alone use this time to gather and pick off solo crew members
-create doubt
-split the crew up
-use your perk to murder returning crew

The Chaplain

Pray for the crew, offer guidence in dark times, good at finding supplies and can dig up graves. Provides moral support for the crew.

Starting item: Shovel, Key

Primary:
Collect and carry supplies
Keep group on goal
Remind the crew of GOD

Secondary: cooking/crafting/navigating

Perk: more likely to find trinkets, can dig up supplies with shovel
has key to the Brig and Lockboxes

Thrall Tactics

-remain dishonest about your findings
-secretly pray to satan
-confuse the crew with your strange religous ways
-use your weakness to your advantage- let the crew not think
you a threat
-volunteer to "help" crew, murder them somewhere private

The Cook

A necessary member to keep the crew fed, very helpful in supporting the ship's crew. Is far more experienced at cooking than the rest.

Starting item: Cleaver, Stew

Primary:
Cook up meals for returning crew
Collect meat and ferry supplies
Keep watch of the ship

Secondary: crafting/navigating/steering/collecting

Perk: cooks faster with stove

Thrall Tactics

-poison their precious meal, blame the doctor if he is close
-is often left unattended, but trusted when food provided
-can easily create excuses to leave and gather
-waste fuel in the stove
-use opportunities to sabotage and decieve

The Doctor

A welcome member of the crew, his soul purpose aimed at keeping the crew alive. One of the most useful support, should gather materials to make antidotes.

Starting item: Cleaver, Laudanum, Syringe

Primary:
Keep the crew alive
Aid the crew in their tasks

Secondary: collecting/crafting/cooking

Perk: carried players die slower and healing items are more effective

Thrall Tactics

-easy access to poison and the stove
-use poisoned syringes to kill the crew
-blame the cook for poisoned food
-good at gathering supplies, seperate and divide the crew
-create trust by healing the useful ones

The Engineer

The macguyver of the voyage, handy in a pinch, can carry more than the average with his barrel and can craft quicker.

Starting item: Axe, Coal barrel

Primary:
Keep the boiler running, if they won't bring you coal you'll have to get it yourself
Rally the crew in the name of the mission!
Keep the ones you trust armed once you arm yourself

Secondary: navigating/searching for supplies/cooking

Perk: crafts worbench items faster

Thrall Tactics

-use explosive barrels to destroy the ship
-hoard useful crafting supplies
-sabotage the boiler, blame anyone close
-fail to collect coal
-if discovered early, can create weapons quickly

The Hunter

The ultimate scout, she is good at what she does. Able to supply the group with food quickly. Is able to hold their own in the wild and has been hired to protect the group.

Starting item: Bow, Arrows, Bear trap

Primary:
Supply the crew with meat
Scout and protect

Secondary: cooking/collecting/crafting

Perk: harvest raw meat faster

Thrall Tactics

-slow them down, insist you know a better path
-use chaos to your advantage, a bear trap used correctly can be devestating
-deprive the crew of meat, insist whoever is cooking has taken it all
-strong ability to survive solo
-torment them from afar

For the Queen and Country!

The Navigator

Ever watchful the navigator strives to be the ultimate scout, tracks the crew, navigate the ship. Good for leading expedition in dire circumstances

Starting item: Spyglass, Lantern

Primary:
Scout out dangers
Keep track of the crew
Eavesdrop on conversations

Secondary: collect coal, crafting, cooking

Perk: sprints faster, lantern can provide warmth

Thrall Tactics

-use the spyglass to listen in
-guide the ship into rocks, blames whoever driving for their efforts.
-you are faster than the others, beat them to the nitro
-good at surviving in the wild
-don't be afraid to confront them
